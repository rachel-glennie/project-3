Who We Are

At CC’s, we serve coffee up hot with a side of strange. Our start was simple - we took a bunch of weird stuff and added our loves of coffee and baking. That’s how we got started, and how we’ve kept it.

Our Treats

Each day’s edibles vary with the seasons and whatever we feel like making. Anything can be available from tarts to donuts to nanaimo bars to fresh bread. We occasionally showcase goods from local bakers. Here’s some of our recent creations.

Our Drinks

We serve gourmet coffee using Bridgehead beans and made just the way you want it. We’re currently experimenting with iced beverages in preparation for summer, come by and check them out!

Our Weird Stuff

CC’s wouldn’t be CC’s without its wacky and constantly changing collection. Anything from animal skulls to ancient fossils to roller skates to china teapots can be found here. You won’t believe your eyes!
